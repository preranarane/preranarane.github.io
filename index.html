Prerana Rane

As a Wireless Research Engineer at Intel's Next Generation Standards group, I've been at the forefront of 5G NR development. My contributions span from research and development of PHY layer systems to actively participating in 3GPP 5G NR standardization efforts. Beyond the academic realm, I've applied my skills to real-world challenges. From designing DSP algorithms for biosignal acquisition at Biosensing & Flexible Devices, Intel, to implementing stress level monitoring in Smart Glasses, my work reflects a commitment to bridging theory and practical application. Proficient in MATLAB, Python, and C++, my technical skills are complemented by a deep understanding of 5G Cellular Standards and Wireless Communication. My diverse experience in research, development, and standardization positions me as a valuable asset in driving innovation and solving complex challenges in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
