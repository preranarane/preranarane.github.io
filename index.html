Prerana Rane

As a Wireless Research Engineer at Intel's Next Generation Standards group, I've been at the forefront of 5G NR development. My contributions span from research and development of PHY layer systems to actively participating in 3GPP 5G NR standardization efforts. Beyond the academic realm, I've applied my skills to real-world challenges. From designing DSP algorithms for blood perfusion sensor to implementing stress level monitoring in Smart Glasses. My diverse experience positions me as a valuable asset in driving innovation and solving complex challenges in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

When not immersed in technology, you'll find me contributing to the IEEE Signal Processing Society or organizing conferences that empower and celebrate women in tech.
